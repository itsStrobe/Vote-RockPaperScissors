GAME RULES


This game is based on the anime series Kakgurui (賭ケグルイ), and follows the same fundamental rules as described in the first episode.

Simply put, it is the game Rock-Paper-Scissors with a twist, in which the Players may only chose a among 3 cards that they pick from a pile filled by Voters.

It consists of 5 main phases:

  1. Lobby Phase
  2. Betting Phase
  3. Voting Phase
  4. Lobby Phase
  5. Lobby Phase

LOBBY PHASE


This is a waiting screen to allow Players and Voters to join.

Roles are defined in a FIFO fashion, in which the first two Users to join are Players and the rest are Voters, up to a cap of 20.

In order to advance to the next stage, both Players must simultaneously click the Ready button.

BETTING PHASE


In this Phase, both Players must agree on an amount to bet in that round.

Any Player may Propose a betting amount that does not exceed his current Credits.

VOTING PHASE


This phase allows Voters to select which Card to place in the ballot.

The game does not advance to the next stage until ALL VOTERS cast their vote. If there are less than 20 voters, the Game randomly autofils the remaining cards.

DRAWING PHASE


In this phase Players receive three random Cards from the ballot filled by the Voters.

They may play any of their cards in order to win the round.

RESOLUTION PHASE


In this phase, both Players reveal their Cards to decide a Winner.

The Winner takes the full amount of Credits bet for that round. After this, both players return the BETTING phase. If a Players runs of out Credits in this phase, this results on the game ending, declaring the other player as the Match Winner.

In case of a DRAW, the game returns to the DRAWING phase removing the played card. If Players run out of cards, the round finishes on a draw and the game goes to the BETTING phase.

Any Player may Forefeit in this round by clicking the RETIRE button, declaring the other player as the Match Winner.
